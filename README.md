### EduFreed

*Because African schools deserve better.*

</div>

> A fully-managed school management system built for African schools.
> No hosting. No servers. No IT teams. Just log in and run.

Schools across Africa shouldn't need a tech department to function in the modern world. **EduFreed** strips away every barrier between a school and its operations.

**Core Modules:**
- Student & Staff Management
- Results & Attendance Tracking
- Fees & Payment Processing
- Communication & Notifications
- Zero-Maintenance SaaS

*Built for schools that were told technology wasn't for them. We disagreed.*

</td>
