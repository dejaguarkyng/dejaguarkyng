### EduFreed — *Because African schools deserve better.*

> A fully-managed school management system built for African schools.
> No hosting. No servers. No IT teams. Just log in and run.

Schools across Africa shouldn't need a tech department to function in the modern world.
**EduFreed** strips away every barrier between a school and its operations.

```
  Student & Staff Management     Results & Attendance
  Fees & Communication           Zero-Maintenance SaaS
```

*Built for schools that were told technology wasn't for them.*
*We disagreed.*
